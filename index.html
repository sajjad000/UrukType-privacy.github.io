Privacy Policy - UrukType Keyboard

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

In Short: UrukType Keyboard does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All your data stays on your device completely.

1. Introduction

Welcome to UrukType Keyboard. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your information when you use our keyboard.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

UrukType Keyboard is designed with a "Privacy First" principle. The application does NOT:

  • Collect, store, or transmit what you type
  • Access your personal information
  • Collect usage data or statistics
  • Send any data to external servers
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Track your location or activity

3. Locally Stored Data

The application stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Word Prediction Data: Words and phrases you frequently use to improve suggestion accuracy
  • Language Preferences: Languages you have enabled (Arabic, English, French)
  • App Settings: Such as long-press duration for keys

This data:

  • Remains on your device only and never leaves it
  • Cannot be accessed by us or any third party
  • Can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the application

4. Required Permissions

The application requests the following permissions to function properly:

  • Keyboard Service: To allow the application to work as a keyboard on your device

We do not request any unnecessary permissions such as access to contacts, files, location, or camera.

5. Adaptive Learning Algorithms

The keyboard uses adaptive learning algorithms to improve word prediction accuracy based on your typing habits. All learning processes occur locally on your device without any internet connection or external servers.

6. Third-Party Services

The application does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no external libraries that collect user data.

7. Data Security

Since the application does not collect or transmit any data, your information is completely secure. All locally stored data is protected by your device's security system.

8. Children's Privacy

The application is safe for use by all ages including children, as it does not collect any personal information from any user.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.

10. Your Rights

You have the complete right to:

  • Delete all local data by uninstalling the application
  • Stop using the application at any time
  • Request additional information about our privacy practices

11. Consent

By using UrukType Keyboard, you consent to this Privacy Policy and its terms.
